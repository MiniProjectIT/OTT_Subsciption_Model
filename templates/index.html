About Us

The above project was developed to fulfill a gap in the market which we aim to occupy. We are looking to be a major player in the OTT Subscription space. Providing a great service that none of our other competitors already offer.

  • To create sustainable aggregated streaming service model.
  • Undercut the prices the competing streaming services present individually.
  • Present a fluid website that ensures good user experience.

We propose a combined i.e. aggregated subscription model while undercutting their prices by a large margin.

Having the following functionalities

  • Offering affordable subscription models.
  • Sleek user interface.
  • Multitude of services provided.

Provides the user with a sleek user experience and UI.

Easily accessible website to assist users with their purchase experience.

The project aims to delivers services to a customer at a competitive price point.

Offering a sustainable price model.

  • Sustainable pricing model.
  • Aggregated Structure.
  • Available to the masses.

Several problems with the current Subscription Service Model have been identified and made us create this model.

Dealing with a redundant and cumbersome subscription landscape.

We aim to combat all the problems that have been identified and made available to the masses, and deliver an efficient and effective solution.
